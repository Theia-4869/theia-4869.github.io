About Me

I am currently a senior student (Turing Class) in Peking University, and will enter BLV Lab, NELVT for my Ph.D. in 2023, advised by Prof. Shanghang Zhang. At the same time, I'm also a Computer Vision Intern at OPPO.

Research Interests

My research interests are in the intersection of computer vision and mechine learning, including visual perception tasks, cooperative perception, few shot learning, out of distribution generalization and computational neuroscience, whose goal is to build autonomous driving systems with high accuracy, strong robustness and generalization ability under various environmental conditions.

More specifically, my research interests include:

  1. Vehicle-road Cooperative (V2I) Autonomous Driving System.
  2. Unsupervised Cross-domain Perception tasks.
  3. 3D camera-based Monocular Object Detection and Depth Estimation.
  4. Liquid Time-constant Neural Networks.
